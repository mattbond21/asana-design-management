Art of delegation

Performance potential

Understanding the opportunities for delegation starts with understanding performance potential on the team. This is only possible if the individual is calibrated on their skills and you have developed trust with them.

Performance can be broken down into two areas: motivation and ability. The grid below highlights the individuals behavior: how your team might show up when not supported properly, and your role: how you can catalyze performance for each individual.
